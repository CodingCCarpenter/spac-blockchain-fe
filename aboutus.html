About Us

Tawfiki Energy Blockchain Platform (TEP) was founded by Rodger McKenzie. Prior to Tawfiki Energy Blockchain Platform (TEP), there did not exist any Blockchain platform which allowed consumers to have low cost and easy access to clean energy, clean transportation, clean drinking water, public goods and services of crypto market place, while allowing accredited investors, along with regular consumers to earn interest on their respective asset holdings, both securities and non-securities, by way of a Securities stable token, and a Utilities semi stable token.

TEP intends to solve the problem of mass adoption in Blockchain and Cryptocurrency, by way of real world use case of real world assets and goods and services powered by the Blockchain.
Our work team is built with of the right mix of selected members, advisors, and technology providers.
